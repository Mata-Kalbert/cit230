Receptions

We know you have lots of options when you’re looking for a perfect, yet affordable, wedding venue. As a bride, you deserve a lot of options to create the environment and mood you’ve always dreamed about while staying within your budget. You’ll appreciate having all the following amenities provided free of additional charge with your qualifying wedding package:

  • White linen tablecloths and a wide selection of colorful linen napkins
  • China tableware, stemmed glassware and flatware
  • All-inclusive room set-up including a skirted head table for your wedding party, banquet rounds for your guests, parquet wood dance floor, staging for your band or head table, skirted gift tables, the cake display.
  • Table centerpieces
  • A fully-trained, professional banquet staff with impressive service skills and fine attention to detail for all plated dinners
  • One complimentary, wedding night hotel stay for the bride and groom at the adjoining Courtyard by Marriott
  • Ample, free, convenient self-parking
